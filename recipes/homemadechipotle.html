Homemade Chipotle

Description

In case you don't want actual chipotle and want to make it at home instead

Ingredients

  • corn
  • tomatoes
  • bagel seasoning
  • avocadoes
  • black beans
  • rice
  • teriyaki sauce
  • butter
  • tuna
  • mayo
  • iceberg lettuce
  • cilantro
  • chicken
  • onions

Steps

  1. dice tomatoes
  2. mix tomatoes, cilantro, onions in a bowl and stir
  3. add bagel seasoning to the above mix and keep stirring until you like the taste
  4. smash avocadoes until you get guac
  5. mix together canned tuna and mayo
  6. put cooked rice on frying pan and melt butter on top and cook
  7. add teriyaki sauce and keep stirring rice
  8. cook chicken
  9. slice up the iceberg lettuce
  10. in a bowl, layer rice first
  11. layer tomato, cilantro, onion mix
  12. next layer, put chicken, tuna and guac
  13. final layer put iceberg lettuce

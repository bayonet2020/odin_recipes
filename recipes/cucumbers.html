Cucumber and smoked salmon

Description

This is a simple recipe for cucumber bites with smoked salmon. Great for an appetizer for lunch or dinner, and very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cucumbers
  • smoked salmon
  • avocadoes
  • honey mustard
  • bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. slice cucumbers diagonally until you get approximately 20 slices
  2. put pieces of smoked salmon on top of each slice
  3. cut and mix avocadoes until it becomes guacamole
  4. top each cucumber with guacamole
  5. use honey mustard sauce on each cucumber
  6. top off with bagel seasoning and serve
